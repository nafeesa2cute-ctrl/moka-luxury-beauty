Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What makes Moka products different?

Moka products are crafted with premium ingredients and innovative formulations. Each product undergoes rigorous testing and is designed to deliver professional results while being gentle on your skin.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Free shipping is available on orders over $150 to most countries.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return unused products for a full refund or exchange.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely! All Moka products are 100% cruelty-free and we never test on animals. We're also committed to using ethically sourced ingredients.

How do I find my perfect shade?

We offer a virtual shade matching service and free beauty consultations. You can also visit our shade finder tool online or contact our beauty experts for personalized recommendations.

Do you offer professional discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for makeup artists, beauty professionals, and beauty schools. Contact us with your credentials to learn about our professional program.
